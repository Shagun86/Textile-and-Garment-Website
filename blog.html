General Dyeing Procedure of Cotton Fabric with Recipe

The dyeing process for cotton fabric involves several steps. First, the fabric is pre-treated through a scouring process to remove any impurities. Then, a dye bath is prepared using the appropriate dye, water, and necessary chemicals. The fabric is immersed in the dye bath and gradually heated to the desired temperature. The temperature and time are maintained as per the specific dye recipe. After dyeing, the fabric is rinsed thoroughly to remove any excess dye. Finally, the fabric is dried and finished according to the desired specifications, resulting in vibrant and colorful cotton fabric ready for use in various applications.
